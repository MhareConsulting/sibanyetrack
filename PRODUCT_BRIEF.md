# myTrack — Product Brief for Marketing Materials

## One-Line Summary
myTrack is a real-time fleet telematics platform that tracks vehicles, monitors drivers, and detects fuel theft — so fleet operators never lose sight of their assets.

## Full Description
myTrack is a South African fleet management and telematics platform built for organisations that need to know exactly where their vehicles are, how their drivers are behaving, and whether their fuel is being stolen. It connects GPS devices on vehicles to a live dashboard, giving fleet managers full visibility across their entire fleet from any device.

The platform handles everything from real-time location tracking and speed monitoring to compliance document management (driver licences, PDP renewals) and fuel drain detection. Alerts are sent automatically when something goes wrong — a driver speeding, a licence expiring, or fuel dropping unexpectedly.

## Target Audience
- Fleet managers and transport operators in South Africa
- Logistics companies managing delivery vehicles
- Companies with field service vehicles (technicians, sales reps)
- Any organisation with 5+ vehicles that needs accountability and cost control

## Core Features (for copy/headlines)
- **Live GPS Tracking** — See every vehicle on the map, right now
- **Fuel Theft Detection** — Instant alerts on unexplained fuel drops
- **Driver Behaviour Monitoring** — Harsh braking, speeding, and harsh cornering tracked
- **Speed Limit Enforcement** — Road-accurate speed limits, automatic violations log
- **Compliance Alerts** — Never miss a licence or PDP renewal again
- **Fuel Consumption Reports** — Know exactly what your fleet is costing you
- **WhatsApp Driver Notifications** — Reach drivers instantly on the channel they already use
- **Geofencing** — Get notified when vehicles enter or leave zones
- **Multi-Fleet / Multi-Branch** — Manage multiple depots from one account
